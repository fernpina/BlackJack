 Load up game by pressing play button.

 After play button is pressed, you will be given the option to place a bet.

 Once bet is accepted, dealer will begin to deal cards.

 Cards when dealt, you will be given the option to either stand or hit.

If your total is more than the dealers, you win.

If you hit and the amount goes over 21, you lose.

 If you stand and the dealers amount is more than your amount, you lose.
